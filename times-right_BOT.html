Victoria Schenck is a weaver, ceramicist, poet, and “prop comedienne” based in Philadelphia. Her work is a casserole of canned images, mundane objects, and readymade materials that over-expose forces hidden in plain sight. She holds a BFA in Fibers from Massachusetts College of Art and Design.
